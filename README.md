Goal

To design a simple and user-friendly fitness tracking application that helps students maintain healthy fitness habits while managing their academic responsibilities.

---

# Problem Statement

Students often want to stay fit but struggle to maintain a regular exercise routine due to academic workload, lack of reminders, and inconsistent motivation.

The objective of this project was to create a simple fitness tracking solution that helps students stay consistent with their workouts and monitor their progress.

---

# UX Research

User Insights

User 3

- Wants progress tracking
- Prefers simple and easy-to-use apps

Key Insights

- Students frequently forget their workout schedules.
- Academic workload reduces exercise time.
- Reminder notifications help users stay consistent.
- Progress tracking increases motivation.
- Users prefer simple and clean interfaces.
